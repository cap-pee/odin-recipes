Pizza Lava Toast

Description

A simple toast covered with melty cheese and pizza toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1.5 ounces sliced mozarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 ounce sliced pepper jack cheese
  • 5 slices pickled jalapeno
  • 5 slices pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon pizza sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon italian seasoning, divided
  • crushed red pepper, to taste
  • ground black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. Spread butter on both sides of each slice of bread and place on the prepared pan.
  3. Bake, flipping once halfway through, until bread is lightly browned on both sides, about 8 minutes.
  4. Cover the perimeter of one bread slice with half of the mozarella cheese, leaving the middle part of the bread empty.
  5. Top with other slice of bread. Use the back of a spoon to make an indentation in the middle of the bread slices, pushing down firmly to reach the bottom slice.
  6. Add the pizza sauce to the indentation, and sprinkle half the italian seasoning over the top.
  7. Cover with remaning 1/2 ounce mozarella cheese and pepper jack, followed by jalapeno and pepperoni. Garnish with remaining italian seasoning, crushed red pepper and black pepper.
  8. Bake until the cheese is melted and golden-brown, about 3 to 5 minutes. SLice in half and serve.
